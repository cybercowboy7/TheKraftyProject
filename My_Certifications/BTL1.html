Security Blue Team Level One

Obtained: August, 2022

Exam Structure

This was a 24 hour hands-on exam. The exam was extremely challenging due to it being a full on incident response. Once remotely entering the infected network, the clock began and you had 24 hours to complete it. I can't go into detail of what exactly was covered in the exam but it basically was an incident that started via a successful file download from a phishing campaign and escalated from there. The exam really tested not only my knowledge in incident response and digital forensics, but it also taught me how to work and manage my time efficiently in order to find all indicators of compromise etc. This exam was my favorite exam due to the nature of it being 100% hands-on versus my others which were purely theory based.
